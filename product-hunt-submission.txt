DESCRIPTION (500 chars max)
---------------------------
News Cartoon transforms local and trending news into witty editorial cartoons using AI. Simply enter your location or a topic, and watch as current events become satirical comic strips. Built with Google's Gemini AI, it generates custom multi-panel cartoons that capture the absurdity of modern news with humor and artistic flair. Perfect for sharing news commentary with a comedic twist, making current events more digestible and entertaining.

I built News Cartoon because I was tired of doom-scrolling through depressing news feeds. I wanted a way to stay informed while also finding humor in the chaos.

The app fetches real news from your area (or any topic you choose) and uses Google's Gemini AI to create editorial cartoons that capture the essence of each story with satirical wit. Think of it as having your own personal political cartoonist who never sleeps.

What makes it unique:
- Location-based news fetching for hyper-local content
- Three-step AI pipeline that creates concepts, scripts, and final artwork
- Built-in humor enhancement to ensure the cartoons are actually funny
- Rate limiting to prevent abuse while keeping it free to use

Try it with your city or a trending topic - I'd love to hear what cartoons it creates for your news!

Built with React, TypeScript, and lots of caffeine. Open to feedback and feature suggestions!
